Batter Up is a website concept I design for a good friend named Thomas Podschelne, who I had the pleasure of meeting and working with at a grocery store. He was a skilled baker, and skilled is an understatement. Thomas had a dream of opening his own bakery and I offered to design and make him one. Unfortunately due to health concerns he took a leave of absence and soon after was transferred to a different store.

CHALLENGE

Create an ecommerce website that appeals to a variety of people, including a streamlined payment process and developing an attractive visual aesthetic.

RESEARCH

I studied the necessities of e-commerce business to see how i can formulate the structure of the site. Since itâ€™s a bakery, it was fairly simple from the get-go, but my goal was to make the experience as easy and simple for the user.
The site would need to:
- Have a simple easy to use navigation structure
- Streamline menu and ordering into one
- Allow users to view the status of their orders
- A simple payment processing system

MENU DISPLAY

The original menu display featured each category with its respective image and a further click away to view the page for those menu items. I later found this to be unnecessary and scrapped the manu page altogether in place of an online ordering page where users can just pick categories they want and choose from an immediate list.
